How can we help?

How do I make an account?

You can make an account by clicking 'Sign Up' located at the top of the screen in the navigation bar.

Can I edit an entry I have already created?

You are able to edit a pre-existing entry, by clicking the "Edit Entry' link at the top left region of the Entry's page. For a quick addition to your entry page, you can alternatively use the "Extra Information" section on every existing Entry page! You can also delete pre-existing entries by clicking 'Delete Entry'.

Can I edit my profile?

You are able to edit your profile using the Edit Profile button within the 'My Account' page. From there, you can also change your password after clicking on the 'Edit Profile' button.
